About Ambioxera

Ambioxera Pty Ltd is a Melbourne-based pharmaceutical company specializing in biotechnology and the sourcing and supply of serum.

Founded in 2022, Ambioxera is a 100% Australian origin, raw foetal bovine serum (FBS) processor with full product traceability. Based in Southeast Queensland, we are driven to be regarded as the most professional business in the market producing validated, fingerprinted serum products.

Our purpose-built facility integrates the highest technology on the market. Our processing laboratory has efficiency built into every aspect, reducing human input to a minimum and removing opportunity for anomalies.
